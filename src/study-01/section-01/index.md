## Section Overview

The opening chapters of Genesis establish the foundation of all Scripture: who God is, who we are, and what went wrong. This section moves from the wonder of creation through the catastrophe of the fall and flood, tracing God's faithfulness even as humanity repeatedly turns away.

### What You Will Encounter

- God as sovereign creator and sustainer of all things
- The nature and consequences of human sin
- God's mercy and covenant-keeping in the face of rebellion
