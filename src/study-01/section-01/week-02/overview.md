## Week 2 Overview

This week we move from the glory of creation into the heartbreak of the fall. Genesis 2 gives us an intimate look at the Garden and the first human relationship, before chapter 3 confronts us with the devastating choice that changed everything. We then trace the spreading consequences of sin through the first family and beyond.

### Background

Genesis 2 is not a contradiction of chapter 1 but a close-up view focusing on the creation of humanity and the establishment of marriage. The serpent in chapter 3 represents the subtle way temptation often works — not through brute force but through questioning what God has said.
