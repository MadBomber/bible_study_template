## Why This Verse

Genesis 2:7 captures the intimacy of the creation of humanity in a single image: God kneeling in the dust, shaping a body, and then leaning close to breathe life into it. This is not the distant deity of other ancient religions — this is a God who gets close. Memorizing this verse grounds us in our identity: we are dust that has been given breath by a God who chose to make us.
