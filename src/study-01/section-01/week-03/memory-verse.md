## Why This Verse

Every rainbow is a theological statement. God hung his bow in the clouds as a visible, recurring reminder of his commitment to sustain the earth. This verse invites us to see the ordinary world charged with covenant meaning — to look up at a rainbow not just with aesthetic wonder but with the recognition that God has bound himself to a promise, and that promise has never been broken.
