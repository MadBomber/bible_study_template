## Section Overview

From the scattering at Babel, God narrows his focus to one man and one family through whom he will bless the entire world. Section 02 follows Abram's call, his wandering faith, and God's progressive revelation of the covenant that will stand at the center of the biblical story. Here we learn what it looks like to trust a promise you cannot yet see fulfilled.

### What You Will Encounter

- God's sovereign call of one person to bless all peoples
- The nature of covenant — what it means that God binds himself to a promise
- Faith tested, faith wavering, and faith ultimately vindicated
