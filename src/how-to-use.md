## How to Use This Study

Replace this content with practical guidance for participants.

### Daily Rhythm

- Read the assigned passage for the day
- Use the [H.E.A.R. method](/hear-method) to record your observations
- Spend a few minutes in prayer

### Weekly Rhythm

- Begin each week by reading the Week Overview
- Work through Days 1–5 at your own pace
- Use the Discussion Guide with your group or for personal reflection
- Memorize the week's memory verse

### Individual Study

You can work through this study entirely on your own. All journal entries and progress tracking are stored privately in your browser.

### Group Study

If you are studying with a group, the Discussion Guide for each week provides questions to guide your conversation. You can share pages with your group using the email icon in the toolbar after adding members in [Settings](/settings/).
